Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ current_year }}

OptiGain is a financial intelligence platform designed to help businesses track revenue, expenses, inventory, forecasting and operational performance. We value user privacy and are committed to protecting business and financial data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as username and email address
  • Business information including business name and paybill details
  • Financial records such as sales, revenue and expenses
  • Operational analytics used to improve system performance

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for platform functionality and service improvement.

  • Generate financial analytics and business insights
  • Provide dashboards, reports and forecasting tools
  • Improve reliability, performance and security
  • Support payment tracking workflows and operational visibility

3. Payment Tracking

OptiGain supports both automated and manual payment workflows.

  • Paybill Businesses: Transactions may be automatically detected through integration with mobile money systems such as MPesa.
  • Till/Cash Businesses: Payments may be manually confirmed and managed by business staff.

OptiGain does not store sensitive mobile money credentials such as PINs.

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information, including account authentication, business data isolation and secure infrastructure practices. However, users are encouraged to maintain strong passwords and protect account access responsibly.

5. Third-Party Services

OptiGain may interact with trusted third-party providers including:

  • Mobile money providers (e.g. MPesa)
  • Email delivery systems
  • Cloud hosting infrastructure providers

6. Cookies & Analytics

OptiGain may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, maintain secure sessions, improve platform performance and better understand how users interact with the system.

7. Data Retention

Data may be retained while accounts remain active in order to provide platform functionality, analytics and reporting services. Users may request account deletion where applicable.

8. User Rights

  • Request access to stored information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request account or data deletion where applicable

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect platform improvements, security enhancements or operational changes. Continued use of OptiGain constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
