Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any software installation?

No. OptiGain runs directly in your web browser and can also be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Can I manage multiple branches?

Yes. The Enterprise plan supports unlimited branches, centralized reporting and branch performance monitoring.

Does OptiGain support M-PESA?

Yes. Businesses can integrate their own Safaricom M-PESA credentials to receive customer payments directly into their own account.

How can I get support?

You can contact us by phone, email or through the contact form on this page.
